Project Overview

Our Process

Our team of 5 people each had a role, from data selection through final deployment. After choosing the dataset, the ETL process was underway to have clean data for the website. An immediate challenge was the data had restaurant addresses, but not coordinates for mapping. Via API calls, the coordiantes were obtained and the joined with the restaurant data. In parallel to the ETL process, a bootstrap template was selected and customized for our site. Team members then began creating the visuals. The most chanllenging part was merging the JavaScrip and CSS for all visuals. Finally, we deployed to the web.

Data Sources

The restaurant inspection dataset is provided by the the New York City Department of Health and Mental Hygiene (DOHMH). The data includes restaurant inspections, violations, grades and adjudication information for restaurants within the five boroughs of NYC.

The restaurant latitude and longitude coordinates were obtained via the Google Geocoding API
