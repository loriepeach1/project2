About Us

We are a team from Atlanta, Georgia, who are enrolled in the GA Tech Data Science & Analytics Bootcamp. Our team project was to build a website which engaged the user to explore the dataset. Collectively, we have 63 years of experience in data analysis, SQL database management, and product management.

Where did our name come from? As we sat in class and talked about all the foods we love to eat in New York, someone said "pastrami on rye" which then led to us laughing about this project being "data on rye"
